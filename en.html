Did you know?

Israel's state budget is funded by 80%-85% taxes, about 5%-8% from land sales, gas royalties, dividends and fees, and roughly 5%-10% from deficit spending (which grew significantly during the war to around 15%-20%).

Privacy Policy

We do not collect any personal data, or any data entered/received at all.

All data you enter (taxes, expenses, purchases) is processed in your browser only and never sent to any server.

The site uses GoatCounter for anonymous usage statistics only (visit counts, clicks). No cookies, no IP addresses, no personal tracking.

Budget data is loaded from Open Budget (obudget.org) — public information.
